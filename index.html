About Me

The first thing you should know about me is that I don't like writing. I'm a half decent writer, I learned when I was 7 and I've been doing it on occasion ever since. My mother is very proud. However, I don't like writing. My words can soar to atmospheric heights of grandeur. However, I don't like writing. Given that, I'm going to stop writing very soon. Very, very, soon. Perhaps I'll stop writing now.

I didn't stop. Did I fool you? Did you think I was going to stop? I didn't. Gotcha! I can stop though, and I will stop. Again, I don't like writing. I can stop whenever I want to and maybe I will.
